# 🧭 Travel Tip: Paying the Bonaire Entry Tax Before Arrival

Visitors traveling to Bonaire are required to pay a mandatory tourism entry tax. Completing this step **before you arrive** makes the entry process faster and helps you avoid long lines at Flamingo International Airport. This guide explains how the process works and why paying in advance is the smartest choice for travelers.

## Why paying early improves your arrival
- It shortens your time at the airport by avoiding on‑site payment queues.
- It ensures you have the required QR code or digital receipt ready for inspection.
- It helps streamline your arrival during peak travel seasons and busy flight windows.

## How to complete the entry tax online
- The tax can be paid through the official online portal before your trip.
- After payment, you will receive a confirmation—typically a QR code.
- Keep the QR code accessible on your phone or printed out, as airport staff may request to scan it.
