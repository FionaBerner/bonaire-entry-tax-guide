# 🌍 Travel Tips

This section collects practical advice to help travelers prepare efficiently and enjoy smoother, stress‑free trips. The focus is on clear, actionable guidance that applies across destinations.

## Essential tips for any trip
- Prepare required entry documents in advance.
- Pay any mandatory tourism taxes online before arrival.
- Keep digital and printed copies of confirmations and QR codes.
- Check airport peak times to avoid long queues.
- Save important travel links in one place for quick access.
