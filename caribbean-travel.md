# 🏝️ Caribbean Travel

The Caribbean offers diverse destinations, each with its own entry rules, cultural highlights, and travel requirements. This guide helps travelers navigate the region more confidently.

## What to know before traveling to the Caribbean
- Many islands require digital entry forms or tourism taxes.
- Peak travel seasons can affect airport wait times.
- Local regulations may differ even between neighboring islands.
- Weather patterns vary — hurricane season affects some islands more than others.

## Destination‑specific guidance
- Bonaire requires a mandatory tourism entry tax.
- Aruba and Curaçao have similar digital entry systems.
- Some islands require health declarations or customs forms.
